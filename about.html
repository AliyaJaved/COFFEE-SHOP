Welcome to our Coffee shop offering a unique coffee house environment unlike any other.We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that),we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee.If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you.If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body.We offer a delicious variety of coffee that we roast in house made by our professionally trained baristas.
